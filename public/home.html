About Us

Wissenaire is the Annual Techno-Management Fest of Indian Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar

Being one of the most awaited Fests in Eastern India, it’s a Technical extravaganza encompassing a multitude of events in the Scientific, Technical and Managerial domains with a well-rounded collection of Competitions, Workshops, Exhibitions, Star Nites, Quizzes, Colloquia, Lectures to inspire Youth to embark on a journey of technical excellence, creativity and innovation


Why become a CA

It is a great opportunity for students looking to improve your managerial and communication skill set.

It will help you in increasing your contacts with people coming from diverse fields across the country and in developing your network.

It is a rare chance of exposure to thousands of young minds and eminent personalities.

Enhance your leadership skills by becoming the face of your college.

What should a CA do?

Sharing the Facebook posts on your timelines and your college facebook and mail groups.

Publicizing events and workshops of WISSENAIRE in your respective colleges.

To strive to bring maximum participation from your respective colleges.

Being the representative of the college act as a medium for effective communication between the Wissenaire team and your college.

What benefits does a CA get?

As a CA of Wissenaire’20, You will get a chance to work with the team of Wissenaire’20 and to witness your work, yielding amazing results. CA’s will be awarded with points according to the number of participants registered, number of CA’s registered citing one’s ID in reference, online activities like sharing and liking the Wissenaire Facebook posts. (share our fb post -10, for each participant registered using CA referral ID-30, for each CA registered using another CA referral Id-15, share an idea - 50, share a contact - 50 )

They will be further ranked according to the points gained and classified into three categories as follows

  • • Rank 1-10: Diamond CA’s

  • • Rank 11-40: Gold CA’s

  • • Rank 41-100: Silver CA’s

The Prizes or benefits for different classes of CA’s are

  • • Diamond: T-shirt + Free Registration for 1 event + Hospitality (excluding food) + 2 VIP passes for concert + Momento from Wissenaire + Photos of CA displayed in website and on social media + Diamond Certificate from Wissenaire

  • • Gold: T shirt + Name of CA mentioned in website and on social media + Gold Certificate from Wissenaire

  • • Silver: T shirt + Silver Certificate from Wissenaire


Who can become a CA?

Students having an unlimited source of enthusiasm and creativity, and the will to be the difference maker can become a CA. Students enrolled in any college and in any year can apply. So, what are you waiting for, let's get started.
